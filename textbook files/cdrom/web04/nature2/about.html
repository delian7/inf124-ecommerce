About Nature's Source

Founded in 1998, Nature's Source was created to serve those who use alternative healing methods. Offering only the highest quality vitamins, minerals, supplements and herbal remedies, Nature's Source takes great pride in helping people live healthier, happier lives.

Many Companies talk about Customer Service and fail to deliver. Nature's Source exists to truly serve all the needs of their customers. Each location features dedicated on-site therapists, along with knowledgeable staff who ensure that every customer receives the best quality information available. Continuing Education seminars are a regular event at Nature's Source to inform their clients about this constantly changing industry.

Nature's Source regularly conducts independent product quality assurance testing to guarantee their clients are receiving the best therapeutic formulations on the market today. Leading manufacturers and suppliers like Genestra and Thorne & Pascoe are proud to be a part of their product team. These companies and others like them allow Nature's Source to provide a global product offering to their customers.

Nature's Source maintains strong ties with the Naturopathic College of Canada and a network of Medical and Natural Health Practitioners across the globe. Adhering to the most stringent standards of the Canadian Health Food Association, Nature's Source has been honoured by them as a leading natural products dispensing retailer.
